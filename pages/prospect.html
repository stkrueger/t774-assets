Patrol Integration

Unlike other organizations, Troop 774 operates on the Patrol Method. Every scout belongs to a small, autonomous team. They elect their own leaders and plan their own expeditions, building grit through peer-accountability.

The 774 Philosophy: "Adults provide the safety net.
Scouts provide the direction."
