The Expedition Pathway

We Don't Stay Put.

Unlike troops that visit the same state park every year, Troop 774 believes in rotation. From the peaks of Philmont Scout Ranch in New Mexico to the coral reefs of the Florida Sea Base, our program scales with the ability of our scouts.

  • Monthly Wilderness Treks
  • Annual Out-of-State Summer Camps
